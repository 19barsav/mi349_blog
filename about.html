Meet the Author: Savvy Barnes

I am a 21 year old student and software developer. I currently reside in East Lansing, MI, with three cats and a puppy. I attend Michigan Stae University and am finishing up two undergraduate degrees in Computer Science Engineering and Psychology. I am also a graduate student in CSE. Additionally, I am completing minors in Cognitive Science and Philosophy! I am passionate about the environment, LGBTQ+, human rights, BLM, and animal rights.

Currently, I am finishing up an interdisciplinary thesis on climate change and cognitive dissonance, utilizing natural language processing techniques from computer science. In addition, I work on the NASA PeTaL team as an open-source contributer, and am a current intern at the Interoperability Institute.
